Right Panel Layout Options

Choose how the detail/logs panel appears on the Cluster screen

A

Toggle-in Right Panel (hidden by default)

Panel is hidden until d or l is pressed. Appears on the right, pushing the table narrower. Dismissed with Escape.

B

Persistent Right Panel (always visible)

Right panel always shown at ~40% width. Shows a placeholder when nothing is selected. Updates immediately when cursor moves (live preview).

C

Split View: Toggle with Scrollable Panel

Like option A (toggle), but the right panel is a scrollable TextArea/RichLog widget that can be focused and navigated with arrow keys — ideal for long logs.
